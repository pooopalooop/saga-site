Industries We Serve

Sealing expertise where it matters

Each industry has its own operating environments, regulatory requirements, and performance demands. We match you with the right sealing solution for yours.

◆

Aerospace & Space

Seals for flight-critical hydraulic systems, fuel systems, actuation, and environmental control. Materials qualified to AS9100 and mil-spec requirements for extreme altitude, temperature, and pressure.

◈

Medical Devices

Biocompatible seals for surgical instruments, drug delivery systems, diagnostic equipment, and implantable devices. USP Class VI and ISO 10993 compliant materials available.

◎

Analytical & Clinical Lab Instruments

Precision seals for chromatography systems, spectrometers, sample handling, and fluid path components where chemical compatibility and ultra-clean performance are critical.

◘

Semiconductor Processing Equipment

High-purity seals for etch, deposition, CMP, and wet clean tools. FFKM and specialty compounds engineered for plasma resistance, ultra-high vacuum, and aggressive chemistries.

✦

Autonomous Defense Equipment

Rugged seals for unmanned systems, robotics, optics, and mission-critical enclosures. Designed for harsh field conditions including vibration, temperature extremes, and chemical exposure.

★

Energy (Oil, Gas, Solar)

Seals for downhole tools, valves, compressors, solar panel assemblies, and processing equipment. High-temperature, high-pressure, and chemically resistant compounds for demanding energy environments.

⌀

Battery Technology

Seals for battery cell assemblies, electrolyte containment, thermal management systems, and manufacturing equipment. Chemical resistance to battery chemistries and tight dimensional tolerances.

◆

Industrial Automation

Seals for robotic actuators, pneumatic cylinders, hydraulic presses, and automated assembly systems. Engineered for high-cycle durability and precision motion control.

◈

Fluid & Gas Handling

Seals for pumps, valves, regulators, compressors, and piping systems. Chemical-resistant compounds for corrosive media, high-pressure, and cryogenic environments.

Who We Represent

Performance Sealing, Inc (PSI)

PSI is a leading manufacturer of high-performance spring-energized polymer seals for critical applications. Using their proprietary Duron® materials and precision CNC machining, PSI delivers engineered sealing solutions for the most demanding environments in aerospace, medical, semiconductor, and energy industries.
