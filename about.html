How We Work

From challenge to solution

A straightforward process built around your engineering needs.

1

Understand Your Application

We start by learning the operating environment, media, size, pressure, temperature, and performance requirements for your seal.

2

Connect with PSI Engineering

We bring in PSI's design engineering team to provide the solution with detailed drawings and a quotation. We help facilitate communication between engineering teams.

3

Solution Testing

We follow through on the first prototype to ensure testing validates fit, function, and performance before moving to production.

4

Support Through Production

From first article through volume production, we stay involved to ensure quality, delivery, and ongoing support throughout the entire organization.
