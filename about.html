Who We Are

Your sealing application experts

SAGA bridges the gap between engineers who need sealing solutions and the manufacturers who build them. We bring deep application knowledge, industry relationships, and a commitment to finding the right fit, not just the fastest quote.

How We Work

From challenge to solution

A straightforward process built around your engineering needs.

1

Understand Your Application

We start by learning the operating environment, media, pressure, temperature, and performance requirements for your seal.

2

Match the Right Solution

We leverage our material and design expertise to recommend the best seal type, compound, and geometry for your application.

3

Connect with PSI Engineering

We bring in PSI's design and manufacturing team to refine the solution, prototype, and validate your seal.

4

Support Through Production

From first article through volume production, we stay involved to ensure quality, delivery, and ongoing support.

Who We Represent
