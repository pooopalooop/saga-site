Seal Types

What we can source for you

Through PSI, we offer a full range of sealing technologies for critical applications.

◎

O-Rings

Standard and custom O-rings in a wide range of elastomer compounds for static and dynamic sealing.

◆

Custom Molded Seals

Application-specific seal geometries molded to your exact specifications and tolerances.

◈

Spring-Energized Seals

PTFE and polymer seals with spring energizers for extreme temperatures, pressures, and chemical environments.

◘

Bonded Seals

Rubber-to-metal and rubber-to-substrate bonded assemblies for integrated sealing in housings and fittings.

✦

Gaskets

Flat gaskets, profiled gaskets, and flange seals in elastomer, PTFE, and composite materials.

★

Diaphragms

Flexible diaphragms for pumps, valves, regulators, and pressure-sensing instruments.

Materials

Compound and material expertise

The right seal starts with the right material. We help you select compounds matched to your operating conditions.

Elastomers

FKM (Viton), FFKM (Kalrez), silicone, EPDM, nitrile, neoprene, and specialty compounds.

PTFE & Polymers

Virgin and filled PTFE, PEEK, UHMWPE, and other engineered plastics for extreme chemical and thermal resistance.

Specialty Materials

Metal seals, composite gaskets, and hybrid solutions for unique environmental and regulatory requirements.
